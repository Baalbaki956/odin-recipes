Italian Corn

How to make lasagna step by step:

This is a delicious seasoned corn side dish. DELICIOUS. Must TRY NOW!!

Ingredients
  • 1 and a half tablespoons butter
  • 2 and a half whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper
Steps
  1. Heat a skillet over high heat.
  2. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely metled
  3. Stir in corn.
  4. Season corn with italian seasoning, salt and black pepper.
  5. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
